The 9 Planets in Our Solar System

Mercury

The smallest and fastest planet. Mercury is the closest planet to the sun and whips around it every 88 Earth days.

Venus

The second planet from the Sun. It is sometimes called Earth's "sister" or "twin" planet as it is almost as large and has a similar composition

Earth

The third planet from the Sun and the only place in the universe known to harbor life.

Mars

The fourth planet from the Sun and the second-smallest planet in the Solar System, only being larger than Mercury.

Jupiter

The fifth planet from the Sun and the largest in the Solar System.

Saturn

The sixth planet from the Sun and the second-largest in the Solar System, after Jupiter.

Uranus

The seventh planet from the Sun. Uranus has the third-largest planetary radius fourth-largest planetary mass in the Solar System.

Neptune

The eight planet from the Sun and the farthest known planet in the Solar System.
